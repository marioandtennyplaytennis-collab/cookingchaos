Welcome to Cooking Chaos Diner!

Your goal is to prepare and serve as many dishes as possible within the time limit. Speed and accuracy are key!

How to Play:

  • Orders (Queue): These are the customer tickets. Each lists the recipe you need to prepare.
  • Stock Room: Click on the ingredient buttons to move them to your **Current Prep Ingredients** list.
  • Prep Station: This is where the magic happens.
    • **Cook Dish:** Compares your **Current Prep Ingredients** against an active order's recipe. If they match, a dish is added to the Prep Station.
    • **Serve Dish:** Serves a dish from the Prep Station. If it matches an active order, you score points!

Clear the prep area (`Clear Prep`) before starting a new recipe!
